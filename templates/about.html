About Us

This Diabetes Prediction application is designed to help individuals assess their risk of diabetes using advanced machine learning techniques. Our goal is to provide an accessible tool for early detection and awareness of diabetes risk factors.

Developed by Pawanpreet Kaur, this project leverages a Random Forest Classifier trained on a comprehensive dataset to predict the likelihood of diabetes based on key health metrics such as age, gender, BMI, and blood glucose levels.

I aim to empower users with knowledge and encourage proactive health management. For more information or to get in touch, please visit my Contact page.
